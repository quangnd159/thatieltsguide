## Useful expressions

- With regard to
- As far as sth is concerned
- this
- However/Nevertheless (often used at the beginning of the 2nd body paragraph in many essays)
- the former
- The sth above show(s) that
  - this shows that
- In fact
- the truth is that
- another reason/benefit/... is that
- finally
- in conclusion
